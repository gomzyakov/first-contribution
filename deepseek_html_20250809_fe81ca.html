How It Works

1

Find a Suitable Project

We curate a list of projects that welcome beginners and have good first issues labeled.

2

Set Up Your Environment

Follow our step-by-step guide to fork the repository, clone it locally, and set up the development environment.

3

Make Your Contribution

Work on the issue, commit your changes, and push to your fork following our contribution guidelines.

4

Create a Pull Request

We'll guide you through creating your first PR, writing a good description, and interacting with maintainers.
